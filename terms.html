Paris Giveaway

Official Contest Rules & Privacy

Last updated June 2026 · Please read carefully before entering

What you can win

French Atelier is flying our biggest sharers to Paris to experience the city firsthand. The prize is a round-trip flight to Paris for the winner. Entry is free — no purchase is necessary at any stage.

01 Eligibility

The contest is open to participants aged 18 or older who reside in the United States. To qualify you must:

  • Follow @livefrenchatelier on both Instagram and Meta (Facebook).
  • Share our channel with at least 10 friends, and
  • Submit clear screenshots of those shares through the official entry tool.

Employees of French Atelier and their immediate families are not eligible. Where prohibited or restricted by law, the contest is void.

02 How to Enter

You may enter using either method below — both are accepted equally:

  • One group share. Share French Atelier to a group of 10 or more friends at once, then upload a single screenshot that clearly shows it was sent to all of them.
  • Separate shares. Share French Atelier with friends one by one and upload a screenshot of each — a minimum of 10 screenshots.

Entries are submitted directly through the entry tool on this site. No email is required and there is nothing to purchase.

03 How Winners Are Chosen

The participants who share our channel with the most friends — verified through the screenshots they upload — are selected by the French Atelier committee. Sharing widely and honestly is the best way to improve your chances.

Winners are contacted directly — by phone and our official email — in early June 2026 or before. A winner who does not respond within 7 days forfeits the prize and an alternate is selected.

04 What the Prize Includes

The prize is a round-trip flight to Paris for the winner. The flight departs from a major airport convenient to the winner — this is flexible; our representative arranges your flight from an airport near you.

This is a flight prize only. It is NOT an all-expenses-paid trip. The following are not included and remain the winner’s responsibility:

  • Hotel and accommodation
  • Meals, food and drink
  • Ground transfers and local transport
  • Travel insurance
  • Spending money and any personal expenses
  • Passport, visa or any travel documentation and associated fees

The prize is non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash. French Atelier may substitute a prize of equal or greater value if circumstances beyond our control require it. The winner is responsible for any taxes that may apply.

05 Your Uploads & Privacy

The screenshots and contact details you submit (your name, phone number and Instagram handle) are used solely to verify entries and to contact winners. We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.

Your submission is sent privately to the French Atelier team and is never displayed publicly. We retain entry data only for as long as needed to run the contest and notify winners, after which it is deleted. You may request removal of your entry at any time by contacting us through our official channels.

06 No Purchase Necessary

No purchase is necessary to enter or win, and a purchase will not improve your chances of winning. Participation in the contest is completely free.

07 Fair Play

Fake, edited, duplicated or misleading screenshots disqualify an entry. Screenshots that are unreadable, too small or cannot be verified may be excluded from qualification. French Atelier reserves the right, at its sole discretion, to disqualify any entry that appears fraudulent or violates these rules.

08 Liability & General

By entering, you agree to release French Atelier from any liability arising from participation in the contest or use of the prize, to the fullest extent permitted by law. French Atelier is not responsible for technical failures, lost or delayed submissions, or circumstances beyond our reasonable control.

This promotion is in no way sponsored, endorsed, administered by, or associated with Instagram or Meta. We may amend these rules or modify the contest where necessary; the version published here is the governing version.
