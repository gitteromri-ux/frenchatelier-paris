The Itinerary

Three days, lived like a Parisian.

Winners experience the French Atelier signature program — the guided Bastille Day tour, July 13–15, 2026.

Day 1

Mon, July 13 · The Golden Vigil

Classic Symmetry & Symphonic Fireworks

Place des Vosges, a golden Seine sunset, and the national fireworks

Begin at Place des Vosges and the Île de la Cité. A golden-hour Seine cruise past the Louvre and Orsay. Evening: the Concert de Paris, then the national fireworks at 23:00 with the Eiffel Tower ablaze.

Place des Vosges Seine Cruise National Fireworks

⚠ In 2026 the national fireworks fall on Monday evening, July 13.

★ Bastille Day

Tue, July 14 · Official Grandeur

Air Shows, Belle Époque & Grand Opera

The insider’s approach to France’s National Day

Arrive early near Concorde and the Jardin des Tuileries for the military air-show flyover — skipping the dense crowds. On to the Palais-Royal and the gilded Opéra Garnier, then the rooftop terraces of Boulevard Haussmann.

Tuileries Air Show Palais-Royal Opéra Garnier
Day 3

Wed, July 15 · Art, Light & Water

Slow Montmartre & Impressionist Masters

Sacré-Cœur, the Musée d’Orsay, and Seine guinguettes

A slow morning in Montmartre — the Moulin de la Galette and Sacré-Cœur — then the Impressionist masters at the Musée d’Orsay. End on the water at a riverside guinguette as the city slows and the light lingers.

Sacré-Cœur Musée d’Orsay Seine Guinguette
